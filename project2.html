early life

DR. APJ Abdul kalam was born on 15th october 1931 in rameswaram,tamil nadu.He belonged to a simple and hardworking family.His father jainulabdeen owned boats and his mother Ashiamma was a homemaker. Kalam spent his childhood in a peacefull enviorment learning important values like honesty,discipline and hard work from his parents

education

kalam copleted his schooling at rameswaram and later studied physics at st.Josephs college, Tiruchillapalli. He then joined the Madras institute of technology to study Aerospace engeneering.His dedication to learning helped him become a succesfull scientist

career

Dr. kalam began his career at the defence research and development organisation (DRDO).Later he joined the Indian space research organisation (ISRO) where he contributed to India's space programme . Because of his work in missile technology he came to be known as the missile man of India

President of India

In 2002 Dr. APJ Abdul kalam became the 11th president of India. He served the nation till 2007 and was loved by the people of all ages . He was popularily known as people's president

achievements

Dr. kalam recieved many rewards including the Bharat Ratna , India's highest civillian award. He inspired millions of people through his books, speeches and visions for a developed India

conclusions

Dr. APJ Abdul kalam was a great scientist , teacher and leader . His life teaches us to dream big , work hard and serve our country . Hebremains an inspiration for students around the world
